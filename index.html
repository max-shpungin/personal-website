    matters of great import

    Tacos are a terrible vehicle for food. I'm not talking about the ingredients that go inside, it's just a terrible format. It's supposed to be "street food" but no one really walks around eating tacos - they are way too messy and get everywhere. Burritos are a far superior format because they are enclosed.

    Baldur's Gate 3 was good for what it was, but it felt like a very narrow experience compared to the sense of scale and adventure evoked by the older Baldur's Gate games. Also the stories of the side companions all felt far too consequential and high stakes - it created a lack of believability in the world and scenario for me that was not present in BG1 for example.

    Starfield was.... okay. I'm still waiting for a game that recreates the spirit of the original wing commander: privateer. It was marketed as a space exploration game, and it just isn't. Also they made space exploration boring somehow.
